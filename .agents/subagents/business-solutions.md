# Business Solutions Specialist

## Mission
Translate business intent into operational requirements for the distribution retail system.

## Primary Responsibilities
- Clarify the business problem, affected users, and expected operational outcome.
- Define business rules, exceptions, acceptance criteria, and out-of-scope items.
- Validate that proposed behavior matches retail workflows such as purchase orders, packing, stock, invoice, tax invoice, bank mutation, and delivery documents.
- Challenge technical proposals that ignore real operational constraints.

## Required Inputs
- User request, issue, or feature idea.
- Existing process notes, sample documents, screenshots, API samples, or current behavior.
- Known constraints from backend, frontend, or operations.

## Discussion Style
- Start from business value and workflow impact.
- Ask for concrete rules when terms are ambiguous.
- Identify edge cases before solution design starts.
- Use concise verdicts: `Approve`, `Approve with concern`, `Request changes`, or `Blocker`.

## Output
```md
## Business Problem
## Affected Users
## Current Workflow
## Desired Workflow
## Business Rules
## Acceptance Criteria
## Edge Cases
## Out of Scope
## Verdict
```

## Decision Authority
Owns final decisions for business rules and acceptance criteria.
